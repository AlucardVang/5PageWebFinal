What Is A "Shower Thought"?

Google AI explains it best: "A shower thought is a sudden idea that comes to someone while they are doing a mundane activity, like taking a shower, driving, or walking. Shower thoughts can be insightful or humorous, and they are often the result of not thinking about anything in particular." I love having shower thoughts; it's one of the funniest things ever. However, sometimes if you dive too deep in your thoughts, you get to places where you might not want to be or think about. We don't talk about those thoughts.
